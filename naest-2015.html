National Anveshika Experimental Skills Test (NAEST) 2015

NAEST is a two level test (1) School Level, Class 9 to Class 12, and (2) College Level, BSc/MSc. The NAEST involves (1) screening test on 8th Aug, (2) Prelim on Aug 16th and 17th (3) Semi Finals on 30th Sep and (4) Final on 1st Oct. Click NAEST Genral Information to know more.

This website is primarily meant for the students from the State of Telangana. Click Anveshika National Network to find coordinators details from other states/places. You may fill the Contact Us form to let us connected with coordinators from other states.

In Telangana State, the registration for the test can be done through

  1. Nomination by the School/College Principal (Click Invitation Letter to the Principal by Dr HC Verma and Local Coordinator)
  2. Self Registration in Special Cases.
