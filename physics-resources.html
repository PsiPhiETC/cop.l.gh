Concepts of Physics Solutions

The solutions to the HC Verma's Concepts of Physics in pdf format can be dowloaded from Part 1 and Part 2.

The link to Android app containing these solutions are Part 1 and Part 2.

As part of textbook companion project, a group at IIT Bombay wrote computer programs to solve each and every problem of the Concepts of Physics. The solutions and programs are given at www.scilab.in
