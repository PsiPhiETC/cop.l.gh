Workshops by ViBha Anveshika

One Day Introductory Workshops for Teachers

ViBha Anveshika organizes teachers workshop in Telangana state. These workshops are part of the Introductory Interaction Programme (IIP). We already conducted these workshops at Hyderabad, Nalgonda, Warangal, and Mahboobnagar.

One Day Intensive Workshop at Anveshika

We conduct one day intensive workshop at Anveshika for teachers and students. The one-day workshop is dedicated on one of the following topics:

  1. Mechanics
  2. Optics
  3. Waves
  4. Electricity
  5. Magnetism
  6. Thermodynamics
The ViBha Anveshika can accomodate 10 teachers (or students) at a time. The workshops are generally organized on Sundays or Public holidays.

Please use the contact us form if you are interested to partcipate in these workshops. There is no fee for the workshop.

Classroom Sessions for Students

We also conduct classroom sessions for students at school premises. The objective is to give a flavour of experiment based learning. These sessions can be of 45 min to 90 min duration.

Science Camp for Students

We also conduct science camps for students. These camps generally spans over two-three days.
