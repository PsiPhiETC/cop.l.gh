NAEST-2015:Screening

The screening test for NAEST 2015 will be held on 8th Aug 2015 (Saturday).

Time: 2:00 PM to 5:00PM

Venue:
Sardar Patel Hall
Keshav Memorial Institute Of Technology
3-5-1026, Beside Deepak Cinema Theartre Lane
Harivihar Colony Road, Hari Vihar Colony, Narayanguda
Hyderabad, Telangana 500029

General Instructions:

  1. Please carry a pen and writing board to the test centre.
  2. Please check that this question paper contains 10 pages. Also, you will be given a 6in plastic scale.
  3. The answers and rough work are to be written in the space provided in this paper.
  4. The quiz is semi-interactive in nature. You will be shown a video clip of 1 to 2 min duration. Watch videos carefully.
  5. The questions related to the video are to be written in the space provided in this paper. If required, you may ask test coordinator to play the video again.
  6. Test duration is 3 Hours. There are 18 Videos.
  7. Raise your hand if you need any help from test coordinator. You are neither expected nor permitted to talk or take help from other students in the examination hall.
  8. Try to give “to the point” answer to questions. Give explanation wherever required. If you are making any assumption to arrive at the answer then write those assumptions in answer sheet.
  9. Relax and enjoy the test!
