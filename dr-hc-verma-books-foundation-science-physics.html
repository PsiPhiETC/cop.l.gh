Foundation Science Physics: Book by Dr HC Verma

The Foundation Science Physics is written by Dr HC Verma and Published by Bharati Bhawan. There are two books with this title, one for class 9 and other for class 10. The books are written according to the CBSE syllabus for class 9 and class 10. Main features of these books are:

  1. Focus on building concepts
  2. Conceptual clarity through simple language
  3. Activities and examples that can be done by the students at home

The books are designed to give a clear understanding of physics. Wherever possible, activities, experiments and additional exploratory material have been given. The questions at the end of each lesson and in the question bank are of various types. The exercises, the additional exploratory material and the suggested activities meet the requirements of CCE.

The book is available on most of the book stores including online stores Flipkart and Amazon.
