This site is dedicated to the "Concepts of Physics", a book by Dr HC Verma. The site contains resource to improve quality of school physics education in India.

New Book for IIT JEE Aspirants!

As part of our efforts on quality improvement, we came up with a book titled "IIT JEE Physics (1978-2015:38 Years) Topic-wise Complete Solutions" that contains problems and their solution from previous IIT entrance examination. Prime focus of the book is on building concepts through problem solving. The book contains more than 1300 solved problems arranged topic-wise. We are trying hard to get it released by July 2015 and make it available via Flipkart and Amazon. Explore the IIT JEE Physics menu item to know more about this book.
